Lasagna

Description

This vegetarian lasagna recipe is made with meaty shiitake and crimini mushrooms, spinach, and ricotta and mozzarella cheeses. It feeds a crowd and will satisfy both vegetarian and meat-eating guests!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound cremini mushroms, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 pound shiitake mushrooms, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 generous cup chopped onions

Steps

  1. Start by boiling the water for the noodles and defrosting the spinach.
  2. Prep the mushrooms and cheeses while the water boils and spinach defrosts.
  3. Use high-quality tomato sauce canned crushed tomatoes, such as Muir Glen crushed tomatoes with basil.
  4. You can prep this lasagna the night before, keep it covered in the fridge, and bake it the day you want to serve it.
