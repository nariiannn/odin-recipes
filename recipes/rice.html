Rice

Description

The BEST Easy Chicken and Rice Recipe! Learn How to Make Chicken and Rice just like grandma used to make. Stovetop, Instant Pot, or Crockpot.

Ingredients

  • Beef Stew
  • Sunday pot roast
  • potato soup
  • Beef chili
  • Potato Salad
  • Navy bean soup
  • Chicken and rice

Steps

  1. Sizzle the chicken pieces until they are golden-brown and crispy. This releases the flavor into the oil.
  2. Cook the vegetables to soften and sweeten them, before adding the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Stir in the rice and water. Then lay the chicken on top of the rice. This helps the rice to cook evenly at the bottom.
  4. Cover and cook the rice until all the water has absorbed.
  5. Remove the chicken bones and chop the chicken.
  6. Stir the chicken back into the rice.
