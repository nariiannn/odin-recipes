Protein shakes also make an ideal post-workout snack that will refuel your energy and help your muscles to heal after exercising.

Ingridients

  • 1 Cup of milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup of ice cubes
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 2 teaspoons honey

Steps

  1. Add all of your ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and serve.
